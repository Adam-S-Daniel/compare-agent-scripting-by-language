- **CsvParser** handles malformed rows gracefully with warnings (skips, doesn't crash)
- **FilterActiveEmployees** is case-insensitive so `"active"` and `"ACTIVE"` both work
- **ComputeOverallStats** guards against divide-by-zero when no active employees exist
- **WriteReportToFile** creates parent directories automatically
