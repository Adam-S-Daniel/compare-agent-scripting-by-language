**Key design decisions:**
- Config format: `pattern:label:priority` (one per line, `#` comments ignored)
- Bash pattern matching (`[[ == $pattern ]]`) handles glob matching including `**`, `*`, `?.x.*` — no external tools needed
- Empty associative arrays with `set -u`: iterating `"${!arr[@]}"` is safe; `${#arr[@]}` is not (bash bug workaround documented)
- Priority sort: labels collected into assoc array, then `sort -rn | awk '{print $2}'`
