### Commit classification logic

- `fix:` → **patch** bump (1.2.3 → 1.2.4)
- `feat:` → **minor** bump (1.2.3 → 1.3.0)
- `type!:` or `BREAKING CHANGE:` footer → **major** bump (1.2.3 → 2.0.0)
- Highest bump wins when multiple types are present
