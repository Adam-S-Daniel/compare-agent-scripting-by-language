**Key design decisions:**

- The live test (`test_live_provider_returns_process_list`) is decorated with `@pytest.mark.skipif(not PSUTIL_AVAILABLE, ...)` so it gracefully skips when psutil isn't installed
- `filter_by_threshold` uses OR semantics and a single-pass loop to prevent duplicates
- `get_live_processes()` wraps `psutil.process_iter` with per-process `try/except` to handle processes that disappear or lack permission
