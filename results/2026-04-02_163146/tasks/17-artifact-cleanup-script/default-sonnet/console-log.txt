### Key Design Decisions

- **Union semantics**: an artifact is deleted if **any** active policy marks it. Policies are independent and their delete-sets are merged with `|=`.
- **Size policy strategy**: delete oldest-first until under the byte budget.
- **`keep_latest_n` is per-workflow**: each `workflow_run_id` gets its own N-slot window.
- **Dry-run**: identical plan, just `is_dry_run=True` — the `summary()` method prefixes `[DRY RUN]`.
